About Us

Building durable, refined swimming pool solutions since 2010.

Gurukrupa Pools is a Pune-based swimming pool construction and maintenance company specializing in technically sound, durable, and aesthetically refined pool solutions across Maharashtra.

Company Owner Mr. Ranjay Kumar
Company Overview

Precision, reliability, and long-term performance.

Established in 2010, Gurukrupa Pools has developed a strong reputation for delivering swimming pool projects with precision, reliability, and long-term performance.

Our expertise spans residential villas, housing societies, commercial properties, resorts, and institutional developments. Every project is approached with a focus on engineering accuracy, safety standards, and lifecycle durability.

Headquartered in Pune, we serve clients across Maharashtra with a commitment to transparent execution, quality workmanship, and dependable after-sales support.
